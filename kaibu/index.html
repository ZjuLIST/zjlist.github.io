Hello & Welcome!

I'm an Associate Professor in College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University. I'm also affiliated with Institute of Cyberspace Research. My research interests include networking and security. I received my Ph.D. degree from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2013, advised by Bin Xiao and offered enormous help by Shigang Chen. I received my BSc and MSc degrees from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2006 and 2009, respectively, advised by Zhixin Sun. I interned at Motorola (Nanjing Center, China) from August 2007 to October 2008. I visited Simon Fraser University and enjoyed teaching CMPT 300: Operating Systems I there in 2018. I'm a member of the ACM, the IEEE, and the CCF.

And I'm preparing to be a teacher that helps students learn better.
